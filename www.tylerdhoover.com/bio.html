About Tyler

Tyler has been active as a musical and theatrical performer for most of his life, expanding his interest in composition while in high school. Holding both bachelor's and master's degrees in Music Theory & Composition from West Chester University, Tyler has enjoyed work as a freelance composer, singer, actor, and multi-instrumentalist, as well as an adjunct music theory faculty at his alma mater. He also serves as Managing Director at Servant Stage.

Tyler resides in Lancaster, PA with his wife, Sandra, and their five children.
